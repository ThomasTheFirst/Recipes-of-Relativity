
Ingediants:

500g lean beef mince
1 1/2 tablespoon olive oil
1 medium brown onion, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, crushed
1 medium red capsicum, chopped
200g button mushrooms, thickly sliced
400g can diced tomatoes with oregano and basil
80g baby spinach
30g butter
2 tablespoons plain flour
2 cups reduced-fat milk
1 cup reduced-fat grated mozzarella cheese
125g dried lasagne pasta sheets

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1:
Heat oil in a heavy-based frying pan over medium heat. Add mince onion, garlic, capsicum and mushroom. Cook, stirring, for 10 minutes or until mince is brown. Stir in tomato. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to low. Simmer for 10 minutes or until sauce has thickened. Remove from heat. Stir in spinach. Cover. Set aside for 2 minutes or until spinach has wilted. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 2:
Meanwhile, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat until foaming. Add flour. Cook, stirring with a wooden spoon, for 1 minute or until bubbling. Remove from heat. Gradually add milk, stirring to prevent lumps forming. Return pan to heat. Cook, stirring, for 5 minutes or until sauce boils and thickens. Remove pan from heat. Stir in half the cheese. Set aside.

Step 3:
Lightly grease a 7cm-deep, 25cm (base) square baking dish. line the bottom of the dish with lasagne sheets and spoon in 1/4 mince mixture. Repeate layers. Spoon cheese sauce over top. Sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake for 40 minutes or until golden and pasta tender. Stand for 10 minutes. Serve.
