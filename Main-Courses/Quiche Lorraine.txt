For 6 people
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 60 minutes

Ingredients
2 sheets ready rolled shortcrust pastry
1 tablespoon olice oil
1 brown onion, finely chopped
4 rashers bacon, rind removed, finely chopped
1 cup grated tasty cheese
3 eggs
1 teaspoon plain flour
300ml cream
1/2 cup milk


Step  1: Preheat oven to 200°C. 
Step  2: Lightly grease a fluted 3cm deep, 23cm (base), loose-base flan pan.
Step  3: Line base and sides of flan with pastry. Trim excess.
Step  4: Line pastry with baking paper. Half-fill with dried beans or rice. Bake for 10 minutes. 
Step  5: Remove paper and beans. Bake a further 10 minutes or until golden.
Step  6: Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Add onion and bacon. Cook for 3 minutes. Drain on paper towels. Cool. Sprinkle over pastry. Top with cheese.
Step  7: Reduce oven temperature to 180°C. 
Step  8: Whisk remaining ingredients and salt and pepper in a jug. Pour over bacon. 
Step  9: Place onto tray. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until set. 
Step 10: Stand for 5 minutes. Serve.
