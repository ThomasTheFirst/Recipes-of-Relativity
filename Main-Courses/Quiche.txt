For 4 people
Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 55 minutes

Ingredients
2 sheets Pampas Reduced-Fat Puff Pastry
1 tablespoon olive oil
125g button mushrooms, slice
2 small carrots, grated
2 small zucchini, grated
350g broccoli, cut into florets
2 green onions, thinly sliced
5 eggs
3/4 cup reduced-fat milk
1 1/4 cups grated reduced-fat cheese


Step  1: Preheat oven to 200°C. Place a baking tray into oven.
Step  2: Line a 3.5cm deep, 23cm (base) loose-base fluted quiche pan with pastry. Trim. Prick base with a fork.
Step  3: Line base and sides of flan with pastry. Trim excess.
Step  4: Line pastry with baking paper. Half-fill with dried beans or rice. Bake for 10 minutes. 
Step  5: Remove paper and beans. Bake a further 10 minutes or until golden.
Step  6: Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms. Cook for 4 minutes. Add remaining vegetables. Cook for 2 minutes. Cool.
Step  7: Reduce oven temperature to 180°C.
Step  8: Spread vegetable mixture over pastry. Sprinkle with cheese. 
Step  8: Whisk eggs and milk in a jug. Season with salt and pepper. Pour over vegetable mixture. 
Step  9: Place onto tray. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until set. 
Step 10: Stand for 10 minutes. Serve.
